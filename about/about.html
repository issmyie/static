About This Project

A fan-made gallery.

Why This Gallery?

The purpose of this gallery is to help you catch a glimpse of my beautiful cats, rob the bunny and bob the old guy.

Color Palette

I wanted light pastels for a dreamy-ish look — #9c89b8 (lavender), #f0a6ca (blush), #efc3e6 (soft pink), #f0e6ef (cream), and #b8bedd (periwinkle). These are all the colors that I used!

Typography

I used Optima, I think it's a pretty font.

Layout & Interaction

I used a pretty basic design to make it easy on the eyes and to navigate.

I love my cats a lot and wanted any excuse to rave about them, especially Minty. — Minty's biggest fan
